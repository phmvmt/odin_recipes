Banana Pancakes

Description

A recipe for the most delicious and healthy pancakes using only 3 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole riped banana
  • 150g oat flour
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Put all of the ingredients into a blender and let it blend until it all mixes together.
  2. The dough should be thick but still a little liquidy.
  3. On a pen on medium heat, form little pancakes until cooked.
  4. Serve however you'd like.
  5. Voila, it's done. Enjoy!
