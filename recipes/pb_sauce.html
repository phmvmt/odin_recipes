Peanut Butter Sauce

Description

The best ever peanut butter sauce that you can mix with noodles, stir fry with rice or use as a dip for fresh spring rolls.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbl peanut butter
  • 1 tbl soy sauce
  • 1 tbl sriracha (or more)
  • 2 tbl water
  • 1 tsb maple syrup

Steps

  1. Into the bowl put all of the ingredients above together and stir until mixed well together.
