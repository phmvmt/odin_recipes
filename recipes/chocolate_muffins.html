Chocolate Muffins

Description

Delicious and easy-to-make chocolate muffins with chocolate chunks.

Ingredients

  • 220g all purpose flour
  • 100g regular sugar
  • 130ml oil
  • 170ml unflavored kefir
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder

Steps

  1. Turn on the oven and put it on 200 degrees Celsius.
  2. Cut the chocolate into small pieces.
  3. Put all of the ingredients into a bowl and mix it well together.
  4. The final mixture should be thick but still runny.
  5. Put the dough into muffin tray.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes and then take it out.
