Best Ever Tiramisu

Description

Best Ever Italian Recipe for Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs (separate egg whites and yolks)
  • 4 tbl spoons of sugar
  • a bit of salt
  • 500g mascarpone
  • 5cl Amaretto
  • a cup of strong black coffee
  • a pack of Lady Fingers
  • cocoa powder for decoration

Steps

  1. Whip the egg whites and salt into stiff peaks.
  2. Whisk the egg yolks with sugar into a foam. Then add mascarpone, carefully mix together.
  3. Add the egg whites (that you whisked into stiff peaks) to the egg yolk mixture.
  4. Add Amaretto to the coffee and mix it together. Dip Lady fingers into it from both sides, but don't soak it too much, the lady fingers should not get soggy.
  5. Put the soaked lady fingers into a tray and make a first layer.
  6. Put the cream on top of the layer.
  7. Continue with the lady fingers and repeat the process.
  8. The last layer should be the cream. Sprinkle the last layer with cocoa powder.
  9. Put tiramisu into the fridge and let it rest for at least four hours, better if kept overnight tho.
