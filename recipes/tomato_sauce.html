Tomato sauce

Description

A delicious and easy-to-make tomato sauce with minced pork meat that will take just 15 minutes of your time.

Ingredients

  • Four large tomatoes
  • 250g minced pork meat
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cinnamon
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Fish sauce
  • Unsalted butter
  • One large onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Chop the onion finely.
  2. Chop the garlic finely. You can use as little or as much garlic as you would like.
  3. Cut the tomatoes into smaller pieces.
  4. Use a small saucepan and put in on a stove on medium heat.
  5. Put some butter into the pan and let it melt.
  6. Put the onion into the pan first and let it cook for a while. Then add the onion.
  7. When both get golden, put the minced meat into the pan and stir.
  8. Add a little bit of salt into the meat and mix it together.
  9. When the meat is almost done, put the tomatoes into the pan and add salt on each of the tomatoes.
  10. Put a lid on the saucepan, lower the heat and wait.
  11. After a while, the tomatoes should be cooked.
  12. Stir the mixture together and put all the spices in the sauce. You can use as much as you would like.
  13. After it's done, you can serve it with any pasta of your preference.
  14. Put a basil leaf on top of the dish.
